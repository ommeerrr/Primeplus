What We Export

Ethiopia’s finest natural and crafted goods — ethically sourced, professionally packaged, and delivered to global markets with pride.

Premium Spices

  • Turmeric — Deep orange, high-curcumin content from Jimma and Oromia; sun-dried, no sulfites
  • Ginger — Fresh, fibrous, and aromatic; harvested at peak maturity for maximum flavor
  • Red Pepper (Korerima) — Rare Ethiopian cardamom; bold, citrusy, and highly sought after in Europe and Asia
  • Black Cumin (Nigella Sativa) — Grown in the southern highlands; used in traditional medicine and gourmet cuisine
  • Korarima (Ethiopian Cardamom) — Indigenous to Ethiopia; prized by chefs in the Middle East and North America

Specialty Coffee

  • Sidama Arabica — Bright acidity, floral notes, and berry undertones; washed and natural process
  • Yirgacheffe — World-renowned for its tea-like clarity, jasmine aroma, and citrus finish
  • Harrar — Dry-processed, wild berry profile; bold, winey, and complex
  • Oromia & Guji — High-altitude beans with chocolate, caramel, and stone fruit notes
  • Organic & Fair Trade Certified — Traceable from smallholder cooperatives to international buyers

Crafted Furniture

  • Modern Living Room Sets — Sleek, minimalist designs from Vietnam and China; solid wood + eco-laminates
  • Dining Tables & Chairs — Durable, stackable, and weather-resistant for indoor/outdoor use
  • Bedroom Furniture — Platform beds, wardrobes, and nightstands with soft-close hardware
  • Office & Hospitality Furniture — Ergonomic chairs, conference tables, and reception desks
  • Hand-Carved Decor — Traditional Ethiopian motifs reimagined in walnut and acacia wood

Our Export Process — Built for Compliance & Profit

We don’t just ship products — we build export-ready businesses. Our end-to-end process includes:

  • Quality Inspection — Lab testing for moisture, contaminants, and purity (SGS, ISO 22000)
  • Export Packaging — Custom crates, vacuum-sealed bags, and branded labeling
  • Documentation — Phytosanitary certificates, COO, commercial invoices, and ICC compliance
  • Logistics — Door-to-port delivery to Djibouti or Port Sudan, then global ocean freight
  • Buyer Matching — We connect Ethiopian producers with verified importers in the EU, USA, GCC, and Asia

Whether you’re a cooperative, a farm, or a manufacturer — we turn your product into a globally competitive export.
