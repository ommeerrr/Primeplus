What We Import

Premium, certified materials fueling Ethiopia’s infrastructure, industry, and agriculture — sourced globally, delivered with integrity.

Construction Materials

  • Steel Coils & Frames — High-tensile, ASTM-certified steel for bridges, high-rises, and industrial structures
  • Aluminum Profiles & Cladding — Lightweight, corrosion-resistant panels for modern facades and roofing
  • MDF & UV Boards — Premium-grade boards for interior finishes, cabinetry, and commercial interiors
  • Plywood & Australian Hardwoods — FSC-certified timber for flooring, furniture, and structural applications
  • Concrete Reinforcement Mesh & Fasteners — Engineered for seismic resilience and long-term durability

Tools & Safety Equipment

  • Industrial Power Tools — Drills, saws, and grinders from Bosch, Makita, and DeWalt
  • Construction Hand Tools — Wrenches, levels, hammers, and measuring kits with ergonomic design
  • Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) — CE-certified helmets, gloves, safety goggles, high-visibility vests, and respirators
  • Safety Signage & Barriers — Weather-resistant signs and temporary fencing for site compliance

Agricultural Machinery

  • Tractors & Tillers — 20–80 HP models suited for Ethiopian highland and lowland farming
  • Seed Planters & Harvesters — Mechanized solutions for coffee, teff, and maize cultivation
  • Irrigation Systems — Drip and sprinkler kits designed for water efficiency in arid zones
  • Grain Dryers & Cleaners — Post-harvest equipment to reduce spoilage and meet export standards
  • Power Generators & Pumps — Diesel and solar-powered units for off-grid farms

Industrial Raw Materials & Furniture Accessories

  • Chemical Resins & Adhesives — For plywood lamination, MDF bonding, and composite manufacturing
  • Metals & Alloys — Aluminum ingots, copper wire, and steel scraps for local foundries
  • Furniture Hardware — Hinges, drawer slides, handles, and connectors from Germany, China, and Italy
  • Wood Veneers & Laminates — Decorative finishes for premium furniture production
  • Plastic & Composite Sheets — UV-stabilized sheets for signage, partitions, and outdoor use

Why Trust Our Imported Products?

Every product is pre-vetted by our global sourcing team, inspected before shipment, and documented with ISO, CE, SGS, or equivalent certifications. We partner only with suppliers who meet international quality and ethical standards — because your project’s safety and longevity matter.
